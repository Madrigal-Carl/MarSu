About.

WELCOME TO THE COLLEGE OF EDUCATION

The MarSU College of Education (CED) is the premier school for Teacher Education in the island Province of Marinduque. The program Bachelor of Secondary Education passed the Level IV – Phase I Accreditation last September 2022, while the Bachelor of Elementary Education is Level Accredited by the Accrediting Agency of Chartered Colleges and Universities of the Philippines (AACCUP). Now, the school with the entire college has been certified ISO 9001:2008 in February 2017 and was awarded ISO 9001:2005 in August 2018.

In the delivery of its services to the people, MarSU College of Education has already attested to itself as an institution providing quality education to the youth of Marinduque. With high passing percentages in the Licensure Examination for Teacher (LET) for BSEd, producing LET Board Top notchers, and high employability of its graduates, MarSU-CFd continuously commits to preparing educators imbued with ideals, aspirations, and traditions of Philippine life and cultures who assume major roles in national and global developments.

For more details and information, please email us at sedboac@gmail.com or visit us at 3rd TLE Building MarSU Main Campus, Panfilo M Manguera Sr. Rd, Tanza Boac, Marinduque.
